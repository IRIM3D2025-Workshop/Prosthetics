Authors' Instruction

Submission guidelines

We invite participants to submit extended abstracts in English, with a length of max. 2 A4 pages, formatted in double column. Word and LaTeX templates are available (https://i-rim.it/en/call-for-contributions/) and only PDF format is available.

Contributions should present innovative results, including recently published work, in all areas of robotics and intelligent machines. The goal is to showcase the main scientific, industrial, and application-oriented activities carried out in the field, highlighting research results and placing particular emphasis on new perspectives and opportunities for our country.

You can submit your paper using the EasyChair website associated with the conference (https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=irim3d2025): in the submission form, the authors will find a field named "Type of submission" and they should select the name of your workshop.

Accepted papers will be invited to submit an extended version to a Special Issue that will be organized after the workshop.

Important dates

  • September 23rd, 2025: Deadline for paper submission.
  • October 7th, 2025: Acceptance notification.
