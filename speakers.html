Prof. Marco Controzzi (Scuola Superiore Sant'Anna, SSSA)

Marco Controzzi is Associate Professor at the BioRobotics Institute, Scuola Superiore Santâ€™Anna (SSSA). His research focuses on the design of artificial hands and advanced prosthetic systems, with a particular emphasis on human grasping and the interaction between human behavior and robotic control. He received his BSc and MSc in Mechanical Engineering in 2005 and 2008, and a PhD in Robotics and ICT from SSSA in 2013. In 2009, he founded Prensilia Srl, a spin-off company that develops and commercializes robotic hands.
